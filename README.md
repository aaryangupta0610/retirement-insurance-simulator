---

##  Overview

The **Retirement and Insurance Simulator** is an educational financial planning tool designed to help users understand:

- Whether they are on track for retirement
- How much they realistically need to save
- If their current investments are sufficient
- Whether their life and health insurance coverage is adequate

The simulator focuses on **clarity, realism, and transparency**, avoiding black-box logic or product recommendations.

---

##  Key Features

###  Retirement Simulator
- Need-based retirement corpus calculation
- Inflation-adjusted, year-by-year retirement sustainability check
- Risk-aware investment planning
- Detection of shortfall (gap analysis)
- Catch-up SIP path generation
- Explainable post-retirement withdrawal strategy

###  Insurance Simulator
- Life insurance adequacy estimation based on income and dependents
- Health insurance coverage estimation based on age, lifestyle, and city tier
- Coverage gap detection
- Indicative premium range estimation
- Simple, rule-based protection logic

---

##  Design Philosophy

- **No hidden assumptions**
- **No product pushing**
- **No black-box “AI”**
- Fully explainable, deterministic calculations
- User autonomy is respected at all times

This simulator is built to **educate**, not advise or sell.
